Student Election Strategies & Tips

Stand out as a strong candidate with these proven strategies:

  • ✅ Develop a clear, inspiring manifesto focused on unity, welfare, culture, and student empowerment.
  • ✅ Engage actively with students through class visits, WhatsApp groups, and campus events.
  • ✅ Leverage social media to share your vision and connect personally with voters.
  • ✅ Demonstrate teamwork and collaboration — voters love leaders who promote Obumu Geemaanyi.
  • ✅ Highlight any previous leadership or organisational experience.
  • ✅ Be transparent, respectful, and committed to delivering results within the academic year.
